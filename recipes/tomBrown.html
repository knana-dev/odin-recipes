TomBrown(Roasted millet)

Description

The image is TomBrown

Ingredients

  • Roasted millet
  • Water
  • Milk
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Pour water into a saucepan and place it over the heat
  2. Add your roasted millet, when the water is warm
  3. Stir till the mixture is free from lumps
  4. Mixture begins to thicken as you stir
  5. Allow food to cook
  6. Dish out your food with milk and some sugar or any other alternative for sugar
