Oatmeal

Description

The image above is a classic image of an Oatmeal with toppings

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Blueberries
  • Sliced Banana
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Pour your water into a saucepan and place it over the heat.
  2. Add some salt to the mixture for taste.
  3. Pour your Oats into the mixture and stir for 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Stir till the mixture is free of lumps.
  5. Allow to simmmer for 3 minutes.
  6. Serve with your Blueberries and sliced Banana.
    7. Enjoy
