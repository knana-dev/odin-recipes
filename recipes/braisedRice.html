Braised Rice

Description

This is a image of an assorted Braised Rice with some veggies; carrots, spring oions, peas, corn and many more. But today we'll learn how to prepare just the meal without the extra stuff.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Egg
  • A tin of Sardine
  • A cup of rice
  • A bottle cooking oil

Steps

  1. Pour oil into a saucepan and lace it over the heat, for some time.
  2. Cut some onions and put it into the saucpan. Allo it to cook for some time
  3. Pour your cup rice into the saucepan and mix everything
  4. Stir and allow the food to cook
  5. Add water to the mixture in the saucepan
  6. Allow food to cook for 10mins
  7. Add your egg to the food and llow to boil.
  8. Check if the water around the found, has formed part of the food.
  9. If yes, reduce the amount of heat cooking the food.
  10. Cover the food with a napkin
  11. Leave food to cook and be checking ocaisionally depending on how soft you want your rice to be.
  12. Viola! Food is ready..Enjoy!
